Search Algorithms



There are two main types of algorithms that we looked at were

  • PageRank algorithm:
    The page rank algorithm was invented by Larry Page, one of the founders of Google. This is the algorithm that google uses, and it is based on how many major websites link to a specific page, that has content related to the search terms used by the user.
