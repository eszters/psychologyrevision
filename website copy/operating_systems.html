Operating systems


Operating systems provide the users with a way of interacting with their computers,by creating an easily understandable outlook of it for the user.


Some of the tasks that operating systems take care of are:

  • the memory of the computer
  • the screen going dim after some time, to reduce battery use
  • displaying the time
  • displaying the battery use
  • networking
  • creating icons for applications
  • moving the mouse across the screen
  • activating the letters pressed on the keyboard


The three most commonly known operating systems for computers are Windows, Linux and MacOS.
